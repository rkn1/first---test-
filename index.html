What Is a Business Purpose Loan?

Educational Information for Business Owners & Real Estate Investors

Understanding Business Purpose Financing

A business purpose loan is financing used for a business or investment objective rather than personal, family, or household purposes. These loans are commonly secured by commercial or investment real estate and are often used by business owners and real estate investors.

Common Business Uses
  • Purchasing investment property
  • Business expansion
  • Debt consolidation
  • Property improvements
  • Working capital
  • Refinancing existing business debt

Who Uses Business Purpose Loans?

Business purpose financing is commonly used by entrepreneurs, LLCs, commercial property owners, and experienced real estate investors seeking financing secured by real estate.

What Information Is Typically Requested?

  • Property information
  • Business information
  • Current mortgage information
  • Purpose of the loan
  • Entity documentation (if applicable)

Need More Information?

Every financing request is unique. Understanding the process before applying can help you prepare the information needed for a smoother transaction.
