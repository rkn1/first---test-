Commercial Bridge Loans

Commercial bridge loans provide short-term financing for real estate investors and business owners who need capital while purchasing, refinancing, or improving commercial or investment property.

Common Uses
  • Investment property purchases
  • Bridge financing
  • Commercial refinancing
  • Business-purpose real estate loans
  • Cash-out financing

What Is a Commercial Bridge Loan?

A bridge loan is designed to provide temporary financing until permanent financing or another exit strategy becomes available.

Who Uses Bridge Loans?

Commercial bridge loans are commonly used by investors, LLCs, property owners, and business operators seeking flexible short-term financing.
