Commercial Bridge Loans

Information for Investors, Business Owners & Real Estate Professionals

What is a Commercial Bridge Loan?

A commercial bridge loan is a short-term financing solution designed to help investors and business owners purchase, refinance, or improve commercial and investment real estate.

Common Uses

  • Investment Property Purchases
  • Bridge Financing
  • Commercial Refinancing
  • Cash-Out Transactions
  • Business Purpose Loans

Who Uses Bridge Loans?

Bridge financing is commonly used by real estate investors, LLCs, commercial property owners, and business operators needing flexible short-term financing.
